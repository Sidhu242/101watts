Top 5 Little-Known Pigeon Facts

Complex Navigation Skills

Pigeons have an extraordinary ability to navigate and find their way home over long distances.
This skill, known as "homing," has been extensively studied and harnessed for centuries.

Remarkable Memory

Pigeons possess excellent memory and can recognize human faces.
They've even been trained to distinguish between different artists' works, demonstrating their cognitive abilities.

Color Vision

Pigeons have highly developed color vision, with the ability to perceive a broader spectrum of colors than humans.
This visual acuity is essential for their foraging and navigation.

Parental Care

Pigeon parents share the responsibility of incubating eggs and caring for their chicks.
Both males and females take turns incubating the eggs and feeding the young.

Pigeons in Art and History

Pigeons have played diverse roles throughout history.
They were messengers in ancient civilizations, inspired famous artists, and even earned medals for their service during wartime.
